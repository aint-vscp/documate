# DocuMate Product Demo Script (3 Minutes)

Format: presenter line, operator action, audience focus
Objective: show all five pillars in strict order

## 0:00-0:20 Opening and Stakes

Presenter says:
We are DocuMate. Today, we will prove contract trust in five steps: identity, drafting, validation, marketplace settlement, and reputation.

What to click:
- Open the dashboard home with wallet already connected.
- Keep chain and account badge visible.

Audience should look at:
- Connected wallet state.
- Polkadot Hub testnet context.

## 0:20-0:55 Pillar 1: KILT Identity Verify

Presenter says:
Step one is identity. We verify once with KILT DID and enforce access through on-chain verification logic, not a web2 profile toggle.

What to click:
- Go to Profile.
- Click Check my verification status.
- Show verified badge/state.

Audience should look at:
- Verified indicator bound to wallet identity.
- DID verification prompt/path when unverified.

## 0:55-1:30 Pillar 2: DocuWriter Draft

Presenter says:
Step two is drafting. DocuWriter generates a usable contract structure, then we lock intent by hashing content for on-chain anchoring.

What to click:
- Open Studio.
- Load or create a template.
- Fill title, terms, and pricing.
- Proceed to mint flow screen without confirming yet.

Audience should look at:
- Structured contract content creation.
- Clear transition from editable draft to on-chain-ready artifact.

## 1:30-2:00 Pillar 3: TEE Validation

Presenter says:
Step three is trust scoring. Our TEE validation flow checks digital signature evidence and assigns a Gold, Silver, or Bronze confidence tier.

What to click:
- Open a validated document or trust panel.
- Trigger or reveal validation result.

Audience should look at:
- Tiered output: Gold, Silver, Bronze.
- Validation shown as cryptographic evidence, not manual review text.

## 2:00-2:35 Pillar 4: DocuMarket Purchase

Presenter says:
Step four is monetization through DocuMarket. Purchase executes with a deterministic split: 75 percent creator, 20 percent treasury, 5 percent staking pool.

What to click:
- Open Market.
- Select a template listing.
- Click purchase and confirm transaction.

Audience should look at:
- Price and purchase confirmation.
- Split visualization and immutable payout percentages.

## 2:35-2:55 Pillar 5: Reputation Tag Generated

Presenter says:
Step five is portable reputation. Verified actions generate trust tags, and breach signals become visible for future counterparties.

What to click:
- Open Profile or Reputation panel.
- Highlight generated tags after completed flow.

Audience should look at:
- New trust tags tied to real activity.
- Breach/risk signaling as accountability mechanism.

## 2:55-3:00 Close

Presenter says:
DocuMate - you are what you sign.

What to click:
- Hold on branded closing screen.

Audience should look at:
- Final brand line and full-flow completion.

## Operator Checklist (Before Going Live)

- Wallet is connected and funded for testnet actions.
- Verified and unverified fallback states are both prepared.
- One ready-to-buy template is visible in market.
- One validated document with trust tier is loaded.
- Reputation profile shows generated tags after transactions.
